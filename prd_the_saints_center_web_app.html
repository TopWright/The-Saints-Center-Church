# Product Requirements Document: The Saints Center Web App/PWA ## Project Overview A creative, storytelling-driven web application and PWA for **The Saints Center**, a church focused on "Raising messengers" and "revelation and power." The design aims for an Awwwards-worthy aesthetic: minimalist, glassmorphic, and visually immersive. ## Brand Identity - **Name:** The Saints Center - **Tagline:** The citadel of revelation and power - **Core Message:** Raising messengers; hearing from God; living the best of both worlds. - **Theme Scripture:** Habakkuk 2:14 - "For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the LORD as the waters cover the sea." - **Mission:** To train believers for the work of evangelizing every nation on planet earth with the knowledge of God's glory. - **Vision:** We see the whole earth covered with the knowledge of the glory of God. ## Visual Direction - **Style:** Minimalistic, Glassmorphic, Creative Storytelling. - **Atmosphere:** Deep, theological, supernatural, modern, and global. - **Color Palette:** - Onyx (#0C0B0B) - Deep blacks for elegance and depth. - Ghost White (#F4F4F9) - Clean, airy backgrounds and text. - Prussian Blue (#05204C) - Sophisticated core brand color. - Wisteria Blue (#8297BB) - Soft accents and gradients. - Bronze (#D48021) - Warmth, divine light, and focus points. ## Information Architecture & Page Structure ### Public Views 1. **Home:** Story-driven introduction, vision/mission, service times, and quick links. 2. **Sermons:** Filterable catalog of sermon series (linked to Google Drive/internal storage). 3. **Sermon Details:** In-depth view for a single sermon with notes and play options. 4. **Programmes:** Calendar and details for church events and daily prayer groups. 5. **Contact:** Location (Lagos State), social links (WhatsApp, X, FB, IG), and inquiry form. ### Admin Views (Management) 1. **Dashboard:** Overview of church metrics. 2. **Evangelism:** Tracking outreach efforts. 3. **Follow-Ups:** Managing new converts and visitors. 4. **Disciples:** Progress tracking for the "Raising Messengers" program. 5. **Reports:** Data-driven insights. 6. **Users:** Role-based access control. 7. **Settings:** App and profile configuration. ## Key Features & Functional Points - **Service Times:** Sundays (7:00-9:30, 9:30-12:00), Wednesdays (6:00-8:30). - **Daily Prayer:** Pauline prayers focused on revelation knowledge and minds flooded with light. - **Deep Teaching:** Sections dedicated to accurate, theological, and supernatural content. - **Global Mission:** Visual elements showing the worldwide reach of the gospel. - **Social Integration:** Direct links to WhatsApp, X, Facebook, and Instagram. - **PWA Capabilities:** Offline access for sermons and quick-launch from home screens.
